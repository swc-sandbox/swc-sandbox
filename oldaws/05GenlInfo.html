General Information

  • Title: “Fundamental Groups in Arithmetic”
  • Dates: March 12-16, 2005
  • Location: The University of New Mexico (in Albuquerque, New Mexico)
  • Organizers: Minhyong Kim, Bill McCallum, and Michael Nakamaye
  • The conference poster (jpg, 184KB)

Speakers

  • Richard Hain: “Iterated integrals, Hodge theory, and fundamental groups”
  • Makoto Matsumoto: “Galois representations in fundamental groups and their Lie algebras”
  • Florian Pop: “Anabelian phenomena in arithmetic and geometry”
  • Leila Schneps: “Grothendieck-Teichmueller theory, moduli spaces, and multi-zeta values”
