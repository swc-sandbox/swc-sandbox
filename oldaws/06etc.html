Excursion

  • The Desert Museum excursion will leave from near the lecture hall at about 1 pm on Tuesday and return at about 5 pm. The cost is $5 if you go with the group, $12 otherwise. Sign up with Sandy Braun outside the lecture hall on Saturday or Monday morning.

Banquet

  • The banquet will take place on Wednesday at the hotel, starting at 6 pm. The cost is $15 . Sign up with Sandy Braun outside the lecture hall on Saturday or Monday morning.
