There will be an informal gathering in the ``Sabino'' room of the Marriott Hotel on Friday, March 9, from 7:00 to 10:00pm.

Registration for the conference will take place at the informal gathering and from 8:00 to 9:00am, March 10, in the lobby outside PAS 201. You can get expense reimbursement forms and pay the banquet fee at this time.

There will be evening working sessions on Saturday, Sunday and Tuesday evenings from 7:00 to 10:00pm. These sessions will provide students with an opportunity to discuss their projects with the speakers, as well as a chance for everyone to get more details or clarifications from the speakers. Active participation is strongly encouraged. The sessions will take place in the ``Sabino'' room of the Marriott hotel. (Monday evening is officially free time, but the Sabino room of the Marriott will be available then as well.)

The conference banquet will be on Wednesday, March 14 at 7:00pm in the ``Sabino'' room of the Marriott hotel.
