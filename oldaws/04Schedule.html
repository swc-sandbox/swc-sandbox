Evening of Arrival

There will be an informal gathering in the hotel on Friday, March 12 from 7:00 to 10:00 PM.

Lectures

The lectures will be held in room 2.302 of the ACES Building on the UT campus

Registration

Registration for the conference will take place at the informal gathering on Friday evening and from 8:00 to 9:00am, March 13, in the lobby outside the conference room. You can get expense reimbursement forms and pay the banquet fee at this time.

Evening working sessions

There will be evening working sessions on Saturday, Sunday, Monday, and Tuesday evenings from 7:00 to 10:00 pm. These sessions will provide students with an opportunity to discuss their projects with the speakers, as well as a chance for everyone to get more details or clarifications from the speakers. Active participation is strongly encouraged.

Banquet

The conference banquet will be on Wednesday, March 17 at 7:00 pm in the lobby outside the conference room in the ACES building. The cost to participants is $15, to be paid at registration.

Free Afternoon

The afternoon of Tuesday, March 16 is free time. We have organized an excursion to go to Pedernales Falls stopping for dinner at the Salt Lick restaurant on the way back. Please sign in for the excursion during registration.
