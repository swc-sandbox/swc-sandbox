General Information

  • Title: “Workshop on Diophantine Geometry Related to the ABC Conjecture”
  • Dates: March 14-18, 1998
  • Location: The University of Arizona
  • Organizers: Alexandru Buium, Minhyong Kim, Bill McCallum, and Felipe Voloch

Speakers and Courses

(The links below lead to the corresponding sections of the notes page.)
  • Alexandru Buium: “Differential algebras and diophantine geometry”
  • Henri Darmon: “Frey’s method and hyperelliptic curves with real multiplication”
  • David Masser: “ABC - diophantine approximation aspects”
  • Barry Mazur: “Visible elements in the Shafarevich-Tate group”
  • Lucien Szpiro: “Some cases of the discriminant conjecture”
  • Paul Vojta: “A more general ABC conjecture”
  • Felipe Voloch: “The Brill-Segre formula and the ABC conjecture”

Professional Development Component

  • The focus of the professional development component was on teaching. Cathy Kessel spoke on “Removing the discontinuity between high-school and college” and led a workshop on “Tools to teach intuition”. There was also a panel discussion on lower division courses.
