Lectures

The lectures will be held in the Integrated Learning Center (ILC), room 140, on the UA campus. Here are walking directions.

Evening working sessions

There will be evening working sessions on Saturday, Sunday, Monday, and Tuesday evenings from 8:00 to 11:00 pm in the Salon A, B & C rooms of the Sheraton Hotel (in the Conference Center behind the hotel). The working sessions will provide students with an opportunity to discuss their projects with the speakers, as well as a chance for everyone to get more details or clarifications from the speakers. Active participation is strongly encouraged.

Evening of Arrival

There will be an informal gathering in the Wildcat 1 room of the Sheraton Hotel on Friday, March 10 from 6:00 to 10:00 PM.

Registration

Registration for the conference will take place at the informal gathering at the hotel on Friday evening 6:00 to 10:00 pm and again on campus from 8:00 to 9:00 am Saturday morning in the hallway outside of room 140.   You can get expense reimbursement forms and pay the banquet fee at these times.

Coffee

Coffee and pastries will be available 30 minutes before the lectures start each morning. Coffee and snacks will be available during the breaks.

Banquet

The conference banquet will be on Wednesday, March 15 at 6:00 pm in the Wildcat 1 room of the Sheraton Hotel. The cost to participants is $15, to be paid at registration.

Free Afternoon

The afternoon of Tuesday, March 14 is free time. An excursion to the Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum is planned. Details about the excursion will be available in the registration packet.
