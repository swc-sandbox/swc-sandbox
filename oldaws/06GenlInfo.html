General Information

  • Title: “Computational and algorithmic aspects of algebra and arithmetic”
  • Dates: March 11-15, 2006
  • Location: The University of Arizona
  • Organizers: Fernando Rodriguez-Villegas, Bjorn Poonen, and Douglas Ulmer
  • The conference poster (jpg, 106KB)

Speakers

  • Daniel J. Bernstein: “Integer factorization”
  • Henri Cohen: “Explicit methods for solving Diophantine equations”
  • Michael Stillman: “Computing cohomology in algebraic geometry”
  • Bernd Sturmfels: “Discriminants, resultants, and their tropicalization”
